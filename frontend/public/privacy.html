Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Neurix ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered productivity platform that connects to Google services on your behalf. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data We Access

When you connect a Google service (Gmail, Drive, Calendar, etc.), Neurix requests OAuth permission to access that service. We only access data in response to your explicit requests in the chat interface.

2. Data We Store

  • OAuth tokens are stored temporarily in server memory to maintain your session.
  • We do not store your Google data (emails, files, events) on our servers.
  • Chat history is stored in your browser's local storage only.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your Google data is only used to fulfil the actions you request.

4. Data Retention

Sessions expire after 4 hours. You can revoke Neurix's access to your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions.

5. Security

All communication is encrypted via HTTPS. OAuth tokens are stored in memory only and never written to disk or logs.
